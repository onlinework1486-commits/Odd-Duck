Our Story

Silver Dragon is known for its authentic Pakistani cuisine, high-quality ingredients, and exceptional service. We offer a warm ambiance for family dinners, parties, and special occasions.

Located in the heart of Faisalabad, we bring you the traditional taste of Karahi prepared with fresh butter and desi ghee, ensuring every meal is a celebration of flavor.
